Welcome

CalSPEED conducts statistically significant measurements of these speeds on a varitey of internet carries across California. Test your upload speed, download speed, message delay (latency), and message delay variation (jitter).

Desktop or Mobile

CalSPEED, released by the California Public Utilities Commission (CPUC), empowers end-users with a professional-level, industry-standard testing tool to measure the quality and speed of their wifi or mobile data connection.
